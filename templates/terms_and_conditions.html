Last Updated: 11-02-2025


Welcome to CampusNest, the official residence for students of Devin University. These Terms and Conditions© govern your stay in the hostel and your use of the CampusNest. By choosing to stay in the hostel and using its facilities, you agree to abide by all rules, regulations, and policies outlined below. These rules are designed to create a safe, comfortable, and academically conducive environment for all residents.

Failure to comply with these Terms and Conditions may result in disciplinary action, including warnings, fines, expulsion from the hostel, and legal action if necessary.


1.Eligibility for Hostel Accommodation

1.1 Who Can Apply?

  • Hostel accommodation is exclusively available to full-time students enrolled in Devin University.
  • Part-time students, external candidates, and students enrolled in distance learning programs are not eligible for hostel accommodation.
  • Priority will be given to students coming from distant locations, as per university policy.

1.2 Age & Identity Verification

  • Applicants must provide valid identification documents, including but not limited to:
  • Aadhar Card/Passport/Voter ID
  • University Admission Proof/Student ID Card br
  • Recent Passport-Sized Photographs
  • Any student found providing false information or forged documents will be subject to immediate expulsion and potential legal consequences.

1.3 Hostel Stay Duration

  • The hostel accommodation is granted for one academic year at a time.
  • Students must reapply for accommodation each year, and priority may be given to existing residents based on their disciplinary record and academic performance.

1.4 Unauthorized Stay is Prohibited

  • Only students officially registered with the hostel administration are permitted to reside in the hostel.
  • Hosting unauthorized guests, including fellow students who have not been allotted a room, is strictly forbidden and will lead to disciplinary action.

2. Hostel Admission & Room Allotment

2.1 Application & Selection Process

  • Students seeking hostel accommodation must submit an application through the official hostel website or university office before the specified deadline.
  • Applications will be processed based on availability, eligibility criteria, and hostel policies.

2.2 Room Allotment Procedure

  • Rooms are allotted by the hostel administration, and students can choose their roommates.
  • Room allotments are final and cannot be changed except under exceptional circumstances such as:
  • Medical conditions (with valid medical proof).
  • Safety concerns or harassment complaints (subject to hostel authority approval).
  • Structural issues in the allotted room.

2.3 Failure to Occupy Allotted Room

  • If a student fails to occupy their allotted room within 10 days of the academic session’s start, their allotment will be canceled, and the room will be reassigned.
  • In such cases, no refund will be granted unless a valid reason is provided and approved by the administration.

3. Fees & Payment

3.1 Fee Structure

  • Hostel fees include rent, electricity, water, maintenance, and other applicable charges.
  • Additional charges may apply for Wi-Fi, laundry, extra meals, or late-night study room access.
  • A detailed fee structure will be provided on the hostel website and notice board.

3.2 Payment Deadlines & Modes

  • Fees must be paid before the beginning of each academic semester.
  • Accepted payment methods include bank transfer, UPI, debit/credit card, and demand draft.
  • Failure to pay fees on time will result in penalties, restriction of hostel services, and potential eviction.

3.3 Late Payment Penalties

  • A grace period of 15 days may be provided for late payments, after which a penalty of ₹100 per day will be applied.
  • Students who fail to clear their dues within 15 days will be required to vacate their hostel room.

4. Cancellation & Refund Policy

4.1 Voluntary Cancellation by Student

  • Students who wish to cancel their accommodation must submit a written request to the hostel warden at least 10 days in advance.
  • Refunds, if applicable, will be processed within 15 working days based on the following criteria:
  • Withdrawal before semester start: 100% refund
  • Withdrawal after semester start but within 30 days: 70% refund
  • Withdrawal after 60 days: No refund

4.2 Cancellation Due to Disciplinary Action

  • If a student is expelled from the hostel due to rule violations, no refund will be provided.
  • Additionally, they may be permanently banned from applying for hostel accommodation in the future.

5. Code of Conduct

5.1 General Behavior

  • Students must maintain discipline, politeness, and a peaceful atmosphere in the hostel.
  • Any form of violent behavior, abusive language, or disrespect towards hostel staff will result in severe consequences.

5.2 Prohibition of Ragging & Harassment

  • Ragging, bullying, and harassment of any kind (verbal, physical, or online) are strictly prohibited.
  • Any student found engaging in ragging will be immediately expelled and reported to the university disciplinary committee and law enforcement authorities.

5.3 Alcohol, Drugs & Smoking

  • Consumption, possession, or distribution of alcohol, drugs, or tobacco products is strictly forbidden in the hostel.
  • Violation of this rule will result in immediate expulsion and potential legal action.

5.4 Noise & Disturbances

  • Quiet hours must be maintained from 11:00 PM to 6:00 AM to respect fellow students’ study and rest time.
  • Playing loud music, shouting, or causing disturbances will lead to warnings, fines, or revocation of privileges.

6. Visitors & Timings

6.1 Visitor Rules

  • Visitors must register at the reception and may only visit during the official visiting hours: 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
  • Visitors are not allowed inside dormitory areas. Meetings must take place in designated common areas.

6.2 Curfew & Late-Night Entry Restrictions

  • Hostel gates will close at 11:00 PM, and students must return before this time.
  • Late-night entries without prior permission will result in disciplinary action.

7. Security & Personal Belongings

7.1 Personal Responsibility

  • Students are responsible for securing their valuables.
  • The hostel administration is not liable for any lost, stolen, or damaged belongings.

7.2 Prohibited Items

  • Students are not allowed to keep weapons, explosives, illegal substances, or any hazardous materials in their rooms.

7.3 CCTV Surveillance & Security

  • Hostel premises are monitored by CCTV cameras for security purposes.
  • Security checks may be conducted at any time to ensure compliance with hostel rules.

8. Termination of Hostel Accommodation

8.1 Grounds for Termination

  • A students hostel accommodation may be terminated if they:
  • Engage in serious misconduct (violence, theft, cheating, ragging, etc.).
  • Fail to pay hostel fees despite repeated warnings.
  • Are involved in illegal activities on hostel premises.

9. Amendments to Terms

  • The hostel administration reserves the right to modify these Terms & Conditions at any time.
  • All students will be notified of updates via email, notice boards, or the hostel website.

By residing in the hostel, you agree to abide by these Terms & Conditions©. Violations may result in warnings, penalties, suspension, or expulsion, depending on the severity of the offense.
